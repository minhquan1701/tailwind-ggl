About Google

Google was founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin and it is headquartered in Mountain View, California.

Google is an American multinational technology company specialized in internet services and products like online advertising technologies, a search engine, cloud computing, software, and hardware.

It is the most visited website in the world and it is considered one of the Big Four technology companies along with Amazon, Apple and Microsoft.

The company began as an online search firm, but it now offers more than 50 Internet services and products, from e-mail and online document creation to software for mobile phones and tablet computers and also Google Calendar, Google Maps, Google Drive, Google Docs, Google Photos, Google Translate etc.

They also released multiple hardware products in 2016, including the Google Pixel smartphone, Google Home smart speaker, Google Wifi mesh wireless router, and Google Daydream virtual reality headset.

For more information, visit their official website: about.google or download the app from the App Store or Google Play.

FAQ

Why did Google change its logo?

Google often replaces its logo for a day with colorful graphical representations to commemorate holidays, achievements or birthdays of notable people throughout history.

Why is the google logo gray?

Google pays tribute to the fallen with a Memorial Day homepage and replaces its normally colorful logo with a charcoal gray-colored one, encouraging users to remember those who've died while serving in the U.S. Armed Forces.

Why did Google Maps change logo?

To celebrate its birthday, Google Maps decided to redesign the app on mobile and to make a brand new logo too which is now more minimalistic with the maps background being replaced by a white one and the location pin icon has round edges in signature colours.

What is the meaning of the google logo today?

The Google logo represents how smart they are and how they’re not just a “system,” but far more intuitive and friendly, the bright colors represent the global cultural diversity and have a kind of unifying power.
